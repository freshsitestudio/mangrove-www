## 1. Theme Selection

**Selected theme:** `warm-local` (Local Favourite)  
**Tagline:** Friendly and community-feel — the kind of site that makes people want to walk in.

_Position:_ warmth=`warm` · tradition=`blend` · density=`moderate`  
_Client signals:_ business type `nonprofit` → inferred warmth `warm` × tradition `blend` × density `moderate`

**Fit (from theme metadata):**
- Cafes, bakeries, and neighbourhood food venues needing approachable warmth
- Local retail, markets, and family-run shops where personality drives visits
- Community organisations and family services where comfort matters more than authority

**Anti-fit (when NOT to use this theme):**
- Heritage trades and barbershops where reliability matters more than likability (thorndon-traditional)
- Cool sparse advisor aesthetics (minimal-professional)
- B2B procurement and infrastructure clients (corporate-tech)
- Clients who need multiple swappable colour schemes

**Other candidates Sally considered (top 3, for transparency — not auto-finalized):**

1. `warm-local` (Local Favourite) — warmth `warm` × tradition `blend` × density `moderate` ← **selected**
2. `thorndon-traditional` (Classic Local) — warmth `warm` × tradition `heritage` × density `moderate`
3. `elegant-luxury` (Refined Luxury) — warmth `cool` × tradition `blend` × density `sparse`
