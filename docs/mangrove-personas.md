## Persona 1 — The First-Timer

- **Who:** Just discovered the business. Found via Google, social, or word-of-mouth.
- **Visits when:** Evening research, comparing options before committing.
- **Wants:** Clear answer to 'who are you, what do you do, why should I trust you?'
- **Hates:** Vague copy, no proof, slow load times.
- **Mental model:** 'Are these people the real deal?'

## Persona 2 — The Returning Customer

- **Who:** Already used the business. Coming back to book again or refer someone.
- **Visits when:** Quick check — hours, location, how to book.
- **Wants:** The same 3 facts in the same place every time.
- **Hates:** Having to dig for the same info because the homepage changed.
- **Mental model:** 'Just tell me the basics and get out of my way.'

## Persona 3 — The Researcher

- **Who:** Mid-funnel — comparing this business to 2-3 alternatives.
- **Visits when:** Reading reviews, looking at work examples, checking pricing.
- **Wants:** Work portfolio, social proof, transparent pricing, FAQs.
- **Hates:** Hiding details behind a 'contact us' wall.
- **Mental model:** 'What makes these better than the other guys?'
